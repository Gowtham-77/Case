DOWRY AND DIVORCE

Dowry practices and divorce laws profoundly impact women's lives, often perpetuating financial and emotional abuse. Advocating for stringent anti-dowry laws, equitable divorce regulations, and robust support systems can protect women's rights. Public awareness and empowerment initiatives are essential to combat these issues and promote gender equality.
